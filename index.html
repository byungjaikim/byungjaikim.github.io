Byungjai (Jai) Kim, Ph.D.

I am an AI Researcher at the AI Center of Samsung Electronics, specializing in machine learning and computer vision. My work focuses on tackling challenges in real-world industrial data by leveraging vision foundation models for specific applications and advancing domain adaptation techniques.

Prior to joining Samsung, I was a Visiting Researcher at the F.M. Kirby Research Center at Johns Hopkins Medicine (USA) in 2019. I earned my M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in Electrical Engineering from KAIST (Korea) in 2017 and 2021, respectively, where I worked in Image Computing System Lab. My work focused on developing machine learning and computer vision algorithms for medical image reconstruction/analysis. Additionally, I contributed to projects involving medical imaging metholodgy, gaining comprehensive experience in medical imaging. I also hold a B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from Sogang University (Korea), graduating Summa Cum Laude in 2015.
