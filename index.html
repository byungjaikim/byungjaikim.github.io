Byungjai Kim

I am a staff researcher at Computer Vision Lab. of Samsung Electronics. My research interests are in the areas of Machine Learning and Computer Vision, focusing on the problems of Medical Image Reconstruction/Analysis and Neuroimaging.

I received B.S degree in Electrical Engineering from Sogang Univ. (Korea) in 2015 (Summa Cum Laude). I recieved M.S and Ph.D degrees in Electrical Engineering from KAIST (Korea) in 2017 and 2021 respectively, working at Image Computing System Lab. As a visiting researcher, I spent time at F.M. Kirby Research Center in John Hopkins University (USA) in 2019.
